# Detail And Store Refinement

## Goal

Make deck, player, and store detail screens faster to scan on a phone without hiding useful competitive context.

## Decisions

- Keep the deck session list compact and move tournament result chips beside the record.
- Distinguish champion, second, top-four, and random-prize results by both color and emphasis.
- Add an environment selector beside the player period selector and preserve both filters in player detail.
- Show staff hand analysis only when recorded hand data exists.
- Add deck and environment context to store history while avoiding a redundant match-count label.
- Reduce header, summary, and floating-action-button height, with enough bottom scroll space to clear fixed controls.
